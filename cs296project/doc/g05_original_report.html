The major components of the elevator are as follows,

  1. The system of counterweights and pulleys which helps to drive the elevator up and down a structure of guiderails
  2. Safety features to stop the elevator in case of any mechanical failure
    • Brakes and grooves on the edge surfaces
    • Governor
  3. The mechanism to open and close the doors of the elevator
